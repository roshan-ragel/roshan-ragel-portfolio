About

Academic Excellence and Innovation

Prof. Roshan G. Ragel is a distinguished scholar and educator at the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, where he serves in the Department of Computer Engineering. His research spans computer architecture, embedded systems, and innovative educational methodologies.

With a passion for advancing technology education and fostering innovation, Prof. Ragel has made significant contributions to both academia and industry. His work bridges theoretical research with practical applications, inspiring the next generation of engineers and technologists.

Through his teaching, research, and public engagement, he continues to shape the landscape of computer engineering education in Sri Lanka and beyond.
